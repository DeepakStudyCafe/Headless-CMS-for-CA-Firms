Our Heritage

Founded by Mr. Arvind Gupta, our firm began with a singular vision: to bring a standard of architectural precision to the world of chartered accountancy. We recognized that businesses needed more than just compliance; they needed a strategic partner capable of navigating complex regulatory landscapes.

Over two decades, Arvind Gupta & Associates has evolved into a multi-disciplinary powerhouse. From a small dedicated team to a prestigious firm serving diverse industries, our growth has been mirrored by the success of our clients—from burgeoning startups to established multinational corporations.
